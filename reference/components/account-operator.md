# Account operator

## Purpose

The account operator manages Platform Mesh accounts and maps account lifecycle to kcp workspace lifecycle.

## Runtime role

It creates and reconciles account-related resources used for organization structure, workspace isolation, identity wiring, and service consumption boundaries.
